Model 2：Peking Opera facial makeup

Peking Opera masks are iconic symbols of Chinese traditional theatre, using vivid colors and patterns to convey characters’ traits, social roles, and fates. Red stands for loyalty and bravery, black for integrity, and white often suggests treachery. These masks combine powerful visual design with deep cultural meaning. This 3D model recreates a classic “Guan Yu” mask design, featuring bold red and black color schemes with symmetrical patterns. Users can interact with the model to examine its detailed lines and layered colors.
