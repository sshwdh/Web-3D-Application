Model 1：Blue and white porcelain vase

Blue and white porcelain is a traditional Chinese ceramic art that features cobalt-blue designs painted onto a white porcelain body, then fired at high temperatures. It is renowned for its elegant contrast of blue and white, as well as its delicate, intricate patterns that embody the refined aesthetics and craftsmanship of ancient China. This 3D model is inspired by a classic Ming Dynasty vase, featuring traditional floral and scrolling patterns. Users can rotate and zoom in to appreciate the graceful form and exquisite details from all angles.
